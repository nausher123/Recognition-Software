Why use CalcScore?

As exams come close millions of students around the world practice using past examinations as a way of preparing themselves. CalcScore simply provides students a fast way to calculate and access thresholds on the basis of which they can see what grade they would get on their performance and evaluate their preperation. It allows students to focus on preparation while leaving the calculation to our tool.
